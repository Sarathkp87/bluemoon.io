Create MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. This will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive BlueMoon.

Transfer BNB

You can transfer BNBto your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Ensure use the BEP20 (BINANCE SMART CHAIN) when transfering BNB

Access your wallet on PANCAKE SWAP by clicking ‘Connect Wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.
